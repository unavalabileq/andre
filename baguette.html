French Bulldog

Standard French Bulldog: This is the most common and recognizable type of French Bulldog. They are typically small in size, with a short, compact body and a flat face. They are known for their friendly and affectionate personalities, making them great family pets. However, because of their short snouts, they can be prone to respiratory problems and overheating in hot weather.

Blue French Bulldog

This is a variation of the standard French Bulldog that has a blue-gray coat. They are popular because of their unique coloring, but some people believe that the blue coloration can be linked to health problems, such as skin allergies and deafness.

Mini-size French Bulldog

This is a smaller version of the standard French Bulldog, weighing between 10-18 pounds. They are great for people who want a French Bulldog but don't have a lot of space, as they are small enough to live in an apartment. However, because they are smaller, they can be more fragile and prone to health issues.
